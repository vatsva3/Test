CREATING CUSTOM SOLUTIONS

For over 15 years, Guddge LLC has been delivering end-to-end tech solutions for an array of corporate entities, government agencies, educational institutions, and more.

We are customer-focused and believe in building strong and lasting relationships with our clients. We take the time to understand their unique business requirements, challenges, and objectives. By gaining insight into their specific needs, we can tailor our solutions to provide the maximum value and impact to their organization. We also understand that every business is different, and there is no one-size-fits-all solution. That’s why we take a customized approach to address our clients’ specific IT needs. Below are some examples of how we have worked closely with our clients to create tailored solutions that align with their business objectives.

Energy & Utilities

OVER A DECADE OF ONGOING PARTNERSHIP TO ENHANCE OPERATIONAL EFFICIENCY

Since 2012, we have been instrumental in Sempra’s digital transformation, starting with analytics for smart meter implementation and evolving to a Gas Operations Data Lake in SAP HANA. Our ongoing partnership now focuses on cloud-based data lakes and data science on AWS, emphasizing advanced technology integration and robust data governance for enhanced operational efficiency and informed decision-making.

Government

FACILITATING SEAMLESS DATA INTEGRATION

We provided IT consulting to Buncombe County, focusing on establishing a comprehensive data warehouse. Our expertise in selecting suitable technologies facilitated seamless data integration and efficient analysis. This collaboration created a robust data environment, enabling improved decision-making and operational efficiency for the county.

Transportation

SIGNIFICANTLY BOOSTING OPERATIONAL EFFICIENCY AND DECISION-MAKING

We developed four enterprise dashboards for Amtrak, enhancing fleet maintenance and financial management using Business Objects and other technologies. Our comprehensive involvement from requirement analysis to design and integration of data warehousing, has significantly boosted operational efficiency and decision-making.

Transportation & Education

REVOLUTIONIZING DATA INTEGRATION AND ANALYTICS

Our IT consulting services for First Student revolutionized their data integration and analytics. We harmonized disparate data sources and implemented advanced analytics solutions, providing data-driven insights that significantly enhanced their operational efficiency and strategic decision-making in the transportation sector.

Technology & Finance

SIGNIFICANTLY UPGRADING FINANCIAL ANALYSIS AND DECISION-MAKING

Our engagement with Dassault Systems centered on developing financial reports using Business Objects XI and PeopleSoft Financials. From specifications to development, including currency conversion and management, we ensured the accuracy and consistency of financial reporting. This project significantly upgraded Dassault Systems’ financial analysis and decision-making processes.
